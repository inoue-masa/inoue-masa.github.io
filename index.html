Wordle!!

  • Guess a 5-letter-word! You can try up to 6 times.
  • When a letter and its position are correct, its background turns green.
  • When a letter is only correct, its background turns yellow.
  • When a letter isn't correct, its background turns gray.
Example
When the answer word is "sound" and you enter "north", the display turns as below.
  • "n" is contained in "sound" however its position is wrong, so its background turns yellow.
  • "o" is contained in "sound" and its position is correct, so its background turns green.
  • "r","t" and "h" aren't contained in "sound", so its background turn gray.
